Our Mission

At Jagjit Welfare Trust, our mission is to offer medical aids to patients on a zero-rent basis. We believe that everyone deserves access to the necessary medical equipment without the burden of financial constraints.

How We Help

We provide a range of medical aids including wheelchairs, crutches, and other essential equipment. Our services are designed to support those in need and ensure that medical equipment is accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

Your support can make a big difference. Through spreading the word, every effort helps us achieve our mission. Contact us to find out how you can contribute to our cause.
